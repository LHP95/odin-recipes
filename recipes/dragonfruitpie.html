Dragonfruit pie

Description

Dragonfruit pie heals 20 hitpoints in total, 2 bites each healing 10, and grants a temporary skill boost to fletching by 4 levels

Ingredients

  • Pastry Dough
  • Pie dish
  • Dragonfruit

Steps

  1. Use a pastry dough on a pie dish to make a pie shell
  2. Use a dragonfruit on the pie shell to make a uncooked dragonfruit pie
  3. Cook the uncooked dragonfruit pie on a range to finish a dragonfruit pie
