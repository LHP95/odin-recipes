Admiral pie

Description

Admiral pies are a type of pie that in oldschool runescape that heals for 16 Hitpoints in 2 bites. It is often used to grant a temporary skill boost to Fishing by 5 levels.

Ingredients

  • Pastry Dough
  • Pie dish
  • Salmon
  • Potato
  • Tuna

Steps

  1. Use a pastry dough on a pie dish to make a pie shell
  2. Use a Salmon on the pie shell to make a Part admiral pie (salmon)
  3. Use a Tuna on the part admiral pie (salmon) to create a part admiral pie (tuna)
  4. Use a Potato on the part admiral pie (tuna) to create a raw admiral pie
  5. Cook the raw admiral pie on a range to finish a admiral pie
