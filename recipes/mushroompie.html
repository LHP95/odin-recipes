Mushroom pie

Description

Mushroom pies are a type of pie that in oldschool runescape that heals for 16 Hitpoints in 2 bites. It is often used to grant a temporary skill boost to Crafting by 4 levels.

Ingredients

  • Pastry Dough
  • Pie dish
  • Sulliuscep Cap

Steps

  1. Use a pastry dough on a pie dish to make a pie shell
  2. Use a sulliuscep cap on the pie shell to make a uncooked mushroom pie
  3. Cook the uncooked mushroom pie on a range to finish a mushroom pie
